Each learner’s journey is unique and this course provides a wealth of information, tools, and techniques. To fully harness its potential, you require strategies customised for self paced learning. Here, we offer a roadmap to help you maximise the benefits of your learning experience.

Strategies For Effective Self Paced Learning

Step 1

Stay consistent

The course structure includes lessons, sections, and tests. Break down your learning into manageable chunks and set milestones for completion.

Step 2

Limit distractions

Find a quiet place to study and turn off unnecessary notifications.

Step 3

Set milestones

Set personal milestones, such as completing a lesson or section to ensure steady advancement through the material.

Step 4

Active participation

Though the course is pre-recorded, actively engage with the content. Pause, take notes, and revisit segments to foster deeper understanding.

Step 5

Real world application

Try applying concepts in real world scenarios or in your daily practice. Bridging theory and practice enhances comprehension.

Step 6

Reinforce with external material

If a topic interests you, consider exploring it further with external articles, research papers, or relevant online lectures.
