Dive into the world of Value Based Healthcare (VBHC), a transformative approach prioritising patient outcomes (quality) over service volume (quantity). This lesson introduces the foundational concepts, components, and the significant impact of VBHC on improving healthcare quality and efficiency.

Before You Begin

This interactive lesson builds on the “Getting started with Patient Reported Measures (PRMs)” lesson, essential for understanding the concepts and terminologies discussed here. Ensure familiarity with foundational knowledge from previous lessons for the best learning experience.

In this lesson, we offer a simulated real world VBHC setting to demonstrate PROMs‘ practical application. Discover how patient care can be enhanced through early detection, goal setting, and integrating mental and physical health strategies.
