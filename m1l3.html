Meet your provider – eHealthier

Welcome to a transformative learning experience offered by eHealthier in collaboration with ASLM, your trusted guide to Value Based Healthcare (VBHC). Our course draws upon the collective expertise of a dedicated team of experts in Digital Health, ASLM and PRMs.

The course builds upon the groundbreaking work of Dr. Eddie Price, FASLM, awarded Pioneer of Lifestyle Medicine in 2023. As a driving force at eHealthier and an esteemed author, Dr. Price’s dedication to redefining healthcare resonates through the material you’ll encounter. Let’s take a moment to understand the powerhouse behind this transformative learning experience.

Who We Are

eHealthier is a pioneering entity in digital health, passionately driving the evolution of healthcare through the seamless blend of innovative technology and best clinical practices. Our guiding star? Achieving true value based healthcare.

Our Mission

Our ethos centres on improving health outcomes through innovative technology solutions and patient centric care. By prioritising the individual patient’s needs and experiences, we strive to enhance the quality of healthcare delivery and empower individuals to lead healthier lives.
