Welcome to “Digital Transformation in Healthcare: A Modern Odyssey.” In an era where technology increasingly permeates every facet of our lives, the healthcare sector is experiencing a significant shift. This lesson provides a comprehensive view of the digital advancements revolutionising global healthcare.

We'll explore the transformative impact technology has on healthcare delivery, patient engagement, and delve into innovations like telemedicine, electronic health records, and big data analytics.

We'll also highlight how technology enables accurate patient insights using PROMs and PREMs, enhancing care delivery and supporting the transition to value based models.

Moreover, we'll examine the confluence of digital transformation with lifestyle medicine and value based healthcare. As you navigate this lesson, you'll gain insights into how digitalisation enhances patient care and addresses healthcare trends and challenges. Join us on this enlightening journey to understand and shape the future of healthcare.
