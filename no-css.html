Course Overview

This course focuses on Value Based Healthcare (VBHC) to redefine patient outcomes and provider efficiency, and dives deep into Patient Reported Outcome Measures (PROMs), Patient Reported Experience Measures (PREMs) and their pivotal role in driving VBHC.

With interactive lessons, videos and hands on simulations, you’ll gain expertise to innovate in healthcare. Whether you’re a healthcare professional or policymaker, this course will equip you with the skills you need to make impactful, data driven decisions in modern healthcare.

Join us as we explore the future of healthcare together and empower the leaders of tomorrow in this vital field.

About Us

This course is a collaborative effort between eHealthier and the Australasian Society of Lifestyle Medicine (ASLM), bringing you the latest insights and best practices in modern healthcare.

ASLM

The Australasian Society of Lifestyle Medicine (ASLM) is a health promotion charity working towards improved prevention, management, and treatment of chronic, complex, and lifestyle related conditions. ‘Lifestyle related’ includes environmental, societal, behavioural and other factors.

At ASLM we support an interdisciplinary approach to health and healthcare; shifting away from siloed practice to create a genuinely collaborative means through which complex problems can be solved.

eHealthier

eHealthier represents the convergence of healthcare expertise and technological innovation. As a pioneering platform in the realm of digital health, eHealthier offers comprehensive solutions for healthcare providers, payers, and patients.
