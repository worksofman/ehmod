This lesson is designed to give you a comprehensive understanding of Patient Reported Measures (PRMs), including Patient Reported Outcome Measures (PROMs) and Patient Reported Experience Measures (PREMs). It explores how patient reported data is essential in shaping modern healthcare and improving patient centred care.

Learning Outcomes

Instructions

Move your mouse cursor over each box below to reveal more information.

By the end of this lesson, learners will be able to:

Define PROMs and PREMs

Articulate the definitions, purposes, and differences between PROMs and PREMs.

Recognise the significance of PRMs

Particularly in assessing and improving the quality of care.

Effectively implement PRMs

Implement PRMs within your day to day practice.

Clearly understand patient reported data

Utilise PRMs data to augment current clinical decisions aimed at improving patient outcomes.

Identify challenges with PRMs

Understand the challenges in applying PRMs and proposed solutions.
